Rules and Regulations

English:

1. The website Stat checkpoint verify your answer was created to allow college students to verify their calculated answers by comparing them to ensure correctness.

2. It is wrong to think that using this website will reduce students' intellectual abilities. This is because it only allows them to see the final solution.

3. Students should use it only to verify their answers. Misusing the website in any other way is not allowed, and it is designed to prevent such misuse.

4. The effort students put into solving problems step by step is interesting until they find the final answer. However, repeatedly using the website to get the final answer easily should be avoided, as it will make them lazy.

5. I, K. Sakthivel, the creator of the Stat checkpoint website, am not responsible if students become lazy and stop studying after repeatedly using it.

6. Students should understand their lessons thoroughly and completely before using this website. Otherwise, it will be of no benefit to their learning.

7. There are no fees for students to use this website. It was created with good intentions for community service and to honor the start department.

8. When students use this website to verify their results, they can use it as a tool to enhance their skills.

9. There will be no issues for students using the Stat checkpoint verify your answer website. It is user-friendly and simple.

10. Students should use this website to verify their answers and thoroughly analyze their skills.

11. Besides the Stat checkpoint verify your answer website, students should use various resources to enhance their knowledge.

12. This website was created to help students, so they should use this opportunity properly to improve their education.
