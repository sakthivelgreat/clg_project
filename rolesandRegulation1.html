Rules and Regulations

English:

1. Admin Access Control: Responsible for managing user access rights and permissions within the system, ensuring proper authorization for sensitive data.

2. Data Integrity Officer: Ensures the accuracy and reliability of data inputs and outputs, conducting regular audits to maintain data integrity standards.

3. Result Validator: Reviews and verifies calculation results, ensuring accuracy and consistency with established criteria and guidelines.

4. Compliance Monitor: Monitors adherence to regulatory requirements and college policies, providing guidance on compliance issues.

5. Quality Assurance Coordinator: Oversees quality control processes, ensuring that calculations meet quality standards and performance metrics.

6. Student Advocate: Represents student interests in matters related to result calculation and verification, ensuring fair treatment and transparency.

7. Ethics Committee Member: Participates in ethical reviews of calculation processes, ensuring adherence to ethical principles and standards.

8. Documentation Specialist: Manages documentation related to calculation methodologies, procedures, and results, ensuring clarity and accessibility.

9. Training Coordinator: Organizes training sessions for users involved in result verification, ensuring proficiency in system operation and procedures.

10. Communications Liaison: Facilitates communication between stakeholders involved in result verification, ensuring effective exchange of information and feedback.
