Rules and Regulations

English:

1. "Stats Checkpoint: Verify Your Answer" - This website was created for college students to verify the answers they calculated using their calculators.

2. Students should only use it to verify their answers. It should not be used improperly. If attempted, it won't be possible, as it is designed to prevent misuse.

3. The calculations students painstakingly perform, line by line, are interesting until the final answer is known. However, after using this website to get the easy final answer, avoid repeatedly using it to get the answer, as this is the first step towards laziness.

4. I, K. Sakthivel, who created the "Stats Checkpoint: Verify Your Answer" website, am not responsible if students become lazy and stop studying after repeatedly using it.

5. Students should understand and fully grasp their lessons before using this website. Otherwise, there is no benefit to their studies.

6. There is no charge for students to use this website. This website was created with good intentions, for community service, and to bring pride to my college's statistics department.

7. It is wrong to think that repeatedly using this website will diminish students' knowledge skills. By using it, only the final solution can be found.

8. When students use this website to check their answers, it can be a tool to improve their skills.

9. There are no problems for students in using the "Stats Checkpoint: Verify Your Answer" website. It is user-friendly and simple.

10. Students should use this website to verify their answers and fully explore their skills.

11. Besides the "Stats Checkpoint: Verify Your Answer" website, students should use various resources to improve their knowledge.

12. This website was created to help students, so they should use this opportunity properly to enhance their education.
